About Us

Roofing Expertise, Genuine Care

We know the roofing industry is a racket. That's why we set out to change it. You deserve a company who has exceptional knowledge of their craft while providing the same service you would receive at your local mom and pop stores. And so, Sanders and Sanders Roofing was born.

From small beginnings....

Will Purvis spend hot Mississippi summers in high school on a roof with his stepdad learning the craft of installing roofs. Nearly fifteen years later, he knew he could take the small one man operation and turn it into something that could change the lives of his family and community. In 2016, Sanders and Sanders Roofing would open in Clinton, MS and become officially licensed with the Mississippi State Board of Contractors and a legacy would be created.

Expansion Meets Community

In 2019, Will saw an opportunity to expand from the original location in central Mississippi and opened the second location in Gulfport, MS. When Hurricane Zeta surprised the Gulf Coast in October of 2020, Sanders and Sanders Roofing would stand out as an honest local company in a sea of pop- up roofing companies looking to make a buck.

We are proud to maintain a reputation that allows for over 40% of our monthly business to come from customer referrals.
